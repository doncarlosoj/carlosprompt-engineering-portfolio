# Scientific Paper Summarizer Prompt

## Problem
Researchers spend significant time reviewing long scientific publications.

## Solution
This prompt guides an AI model to extract structured insights from scientific papers.

## Prompt

You are a scientific research analyst.

Summarize the following research paper into the sections below:

1. Research Objective
2. Methodology
3. Key Findings
4. Limitations
5. Practical Applications

## Expected Output
A clear structured summary suitable for healthcare or research professionals.
